About Us

We are the University of Texas at San Antonio student chapter of MAES, a student-managed organization that is designed to help students during their collegiate career and to ultimately get hired in the STEM field. Our chapter has been one of the most successful and nationally recognized chapters in recent history due to our outreach, volunteer work, leadership, and dedication. Our mission is to promote and expand as individuals and as an organization to create opportunities for all STEM minorities in San Antonio. Our vision is to increase and get recognition of the MAES organization for the development of STEM leaders in the academic, executive, and technical communities in San Antonio. We are, to this day, the model student engineering organization and not only at the College of Engineering, but all throughout the UTSA campus.
